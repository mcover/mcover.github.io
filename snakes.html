Snakes



This is a game that I made on a five person team over the course of 2 weeks for CMS.611 Creating Videogames.  On this game I was responsible for Art, UI Design and Implementation, and QA and Debugging.  I also did a lot of the integrating of back-end code with Unity and implementing of the Art.  This game was made in Unity 5 with C#.  The link to an older web version can be found below.

My team members were Nayeon Kim, Forrest Moret, Jorrie Brettin, and Giulio Gueltrini, all of whom are students at MIT.

I wish that we had had more time to develop this game.  Because this was only a two week project, we ended up having to cut features that some people on the team felt were crucial to the game.   Given our time frame, I do feel that we accomplished a lot and captured most of what we wanted from our original design.
